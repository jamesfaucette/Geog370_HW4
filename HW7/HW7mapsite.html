Homework 7: Number of Impoverished People in each Georgia County

JT Faucette

This data consists of a census estimate of the number of people in each Georgia county that live beneath the poverty line. Obviously, in the more populated counties, such as Fulton or DeKalb, the numbers will be higher. However, I still believe it shows just how many people in Georgia are struggling.
